About Sudsy

Sudsy is Hungary's first fully connected laundromat franchise, designed to make laundry day effortless with cutting-edge technology and convenient locations. Our mobile app lets you reserve machines, track progress, and manage payments—all from your phone. With our new AI-powered fabric analysis, you can upload a photo of your clothes, and we'll recommend the ideal wash and dry settings, ensuring your garments are cared for perfectly every time.
